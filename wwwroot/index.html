Key Features

Our app provides a simple and secure way to access Gmail accounts with proper permissions and authorization.

Secure OAuth Authentication

Connect Gmail accounts using Google's secure OAuth protocol, ensuring credentials are never stored or exposed.

Flexible Access Options

Choose between temporary (6-month) access or ongoing access, giving you control over how long the app can read emails.

Read-Only Access

The app only requests read-only access to emails, ensuring it can never modify, delete, or send emails from your account.
